# Volume 1 — Product Vision

**Mileage & Expense Copilot** | Master Build Blueprint v1.0

---

## The Problem

Most people track business travel in three disconnected places:

1. **Notes app** — mileage scribbles
2. **Shoebox or email** — receipts
3. **Spreadsheet** — organized at tax time (if ever)

This is tedious. People miss deductions or spend hours reconstructing records.

---

## The Solution

One app. One workflow. **Start Trip → Add Expenses → End Trip.**

### Before leaving
Starting odometer (or GPS estimate) · Starting location · Destination · Client/project · Purpose · Vehicle

### During the trip
**Add Expense** — photograph receipt → AI extracts merchant, date, amount, tax, payment method → categorize (fuel, meal, hotel, parking, toll, supplies)

### End of trip
Ending odometer · Closing location · Notes · **"Forgot Something?" checklist**

### Automatic calculations
Total miles · Mileage reimbursement · Total expenses · Grand total · Estimated tax deduction

## Differentiation

| Competitor weakness | Our answer |
|--------------------|------------|
| Receipt apps ignore mileage | Unified trip + expense model |
| Mileage apps ignore receipts | Receipt OCR attached to trips |
| Spreadsheets require discipline | One-tap capture, AI assists |
| Accounting tools are overwhelming | Focused travel documentation only |
| Generic expense tools lack IRS mileage log | Built-in IRS-style reports |

**Positioning:** Not "expense tracking" — **"Never lose another tax deduction."**

**Brand:** Mileage & Expense Copilot / Travel Expense OS

## The "Wow" Feature — One-Tap Expense Intelligence

User photographs a receipt. Within seconds:

```
Trip #245 · Fuel · Shell · Tulsa, OK · $63.42
Assigned to: Jones Roofing estimate
Total business trip value: $184.17
Tax deduction updated.
```

User does almost nothing. AI suggests; user confirms.

## Pricing Strategy

| Tier | Price | Key limits |
|------|-------|------------|
| **Free** | $0 | 5 trips/mo, 10 receipts/mo, 1 vehicle, 1 business, PDF only |
| **Pro** | $4.99/mo | Unlimited core features, AI OCR, CSV/Excel, cloud backup |
| **Small Business** | $19.99/mo | 5 employees, approvals, shared dashboard |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | SSO, API, integrations, custom branding |

$4.99 is an impulse purchase — low friction to convert after free tier proves value.

## Version 1 Definition

### In scope
Accounts · Businesses · Vehicles · Trips (start/end/edit) · Receipts (OCR) · Expenses · Reports · Dashboard · Settings · Subscriptions · Trip timeline · End-trip checklist · Basic AI (OCR, categorize, duplicate detect)

## Success Metrics

### Acquisition
- Landing page → signup conversion
- App Store / organic referral (post-PWA)

### Activation
- % users who complete first trip within 7 days
- % users who scan first receipt within 7 days
- Time to first trip < 5 minutes from signup

### Engagement
- Trips per active user per month
- Receipts per trip
- Weekly active users / monthly active users ratio

### Revenue
- Free → Pro conversion rate
- Monthly churn (Pro)
- ARPU

### Quality
- OCR field accuracy (user correction rate)
- Support tickets per 100 users
- Report generation success rate

### North Star
**Completed business trips documented per week** — proxy for deductions captured and time saved.

---

## Product Philosophy Summary

1. Sell time and deductions, not bookkeeping
2. Mobile capture beats desktop organization
3. AI suggests; humans confirm financial data
4. Reports must work for IRS, clients, and employers without training
5. Export everything — trust through portability

---

## Long-Term Vision (Post-V1)

Evolve from mileage tracker → **AI assistant for business travel intelligence:**

- "Where is my money going?"
- "How much should I bill this client?"
- "What deductions am I missing?"
- "Which customers cost the most to service?"
- "How profitable are trips after fuel, tolls, and meals?"

V1 lays the data foundation (trips, receipts, categories, clients) that powers this future.

---

## Competitive Moat (Over Time)

- Trip + receipt unified timeline (hard to replicate piecemeal)
- AI trained on travel-specific receipt patterns
- Client/project profitability from travel data
- Habit-forming "Forgot Something?" workflow
- Tax-ready exports without accountant translation
