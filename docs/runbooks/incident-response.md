# Incident Response Runbook

**OPS-RB-001** · Volume 19 Ch. 13 · Volume 17 Ch. 22 · Volume 8 Ch. 19

## Severity

| Level | Response time | Examples |
|-------|---------------|----------|
| P1 | 15 min | Auth down, data loss |
| P2 | 1 hour | OCR > 50% failure |
| P3 | 4 hours | Report generation degraded |
| P4 | Next business day | Minor issues |

## Workflow

1. Detect (monitoring / customer report)
2. Assign severity and owner
3. Assess customer impact
4. Contain and resolve
5. Post-incident review within 48h (P1–P2)
